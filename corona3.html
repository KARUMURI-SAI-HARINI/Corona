CORONA VIRUS

-Originated in bats or pangolins

-The first known cases of COVID-19

    were in Wuhan, CHINA.

Shape

-Large,roughly spherical,particles with bulbous surface projections

-Average diameter of the virus particles is around 125 nm

Discovered at:-

-Coronaviruses were first discovered in the 1930s,

  when an acute respiratory infection of domesticated

  chickens was shown to be caused by infectious bronchitis virus

Family of COVID-19

-Coronaviruses constitute the subfamily of

  Orthocoronavirinae,in the family Coronaviridae,

  order Nidovirales,and realm Riboviria
